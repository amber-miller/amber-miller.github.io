    Personal Projects

    Dice Box & Pill Dispenser

    2021

    A passing interest in additive manufacturing bloomed into a passion during my mechanical engineering degree course for engineering design. During this semster students were given a cheap 3D printer to assembly and use to fullfill the course. Starting with small design projects like a dice box as a gift for family lead to more complex projects and excitement for design. The automoated pillbox was designed to fill a need to dispense pills regularly with an alarm reminder to alert the user. The alarm turns off once pills are no longer sensed in its dish and is phone adpatable to send refill reminders for the user or caregiver. The pillbox was made with the idea for expansion, being removable in sections and each section linking electronically to the main electronics hidden it in base.

    The pillbox was the first project to merge electronic and mechanical design. This pillbox was meant to fill a need of those taking medication and replace expensive medical devices sold on the market today.
